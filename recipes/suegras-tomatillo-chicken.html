Suegra's Tomatillo Chicken

Description

Chicken pieces are seared and then simmered in a tomatillo sauce. This is a mother in law's recipe from the state of Zacatecas, Mexico. Serve with warm tortillas and your choice of rice or beans as a side. We recommend a cold cola pop to accompany your delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 1/2) pound whole chicken, cut into 6 pieces
  • 1 pound fresh tomatillos, husks removed
  • 2 dried California chile pods
  • 3 dried red chile peppers
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (200 degrees C). Place the tomatillos, California chiles, and red chile peppers into a small roasting pan or a rimmed baking sheet. Roast for about 20 minutes, turning frequently. If the chiles start to get too dark, remove them and place into a food processor or blender.
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken pieces and cook, turning to sear evely on all sides.
  3. Combine the tomatillos and chilies in a blender or food processor and blend until smooth. Season with salt to taste. Pour this mixture in with the chicken. Reduce heat to meadium-low, cover and simmer until chicken is tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
